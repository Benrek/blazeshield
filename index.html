BlazeShield and Safety Solutions is one of Mombasa's largest and fastest-growing fire safety and supply companies, offering a comprehensive range of firefighting products, equipment, and technological solutions.

Led by professionals with extensive national and international experience, BlazeShield and Safety Solutions provides expert consultancy services.

Our fire fighting solutions and services

  • Fire safety consultancy and training. .
  • First aid training.
  • Installation of Fire Extinguishers & Hose Reels at Offices and Residential Houses
  • Supply and maintenance of firefighting equipment.
  • Supply and training on the use of Personal Protective equipment (PPE).
  • Supply of first aid equipment
  • Fire Suppression Systems
  • Kitchen Fire Suppression
  • Fire Detection & Alarm Systems
  • Voice Evacuation Systems
